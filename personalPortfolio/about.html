About Me

I grew up in the beautiful Meridian Idaho where I learned to love my community. I decided to pursue a career in software development because I see how passionate the community can be! I bring to the table technical trouble-shooting skills and I have a love of learning. Additionally, I am a team player with excellent communication abilities. I am career driven, and am highly self-motivated and I pride myself as a professional. After spending 6 years in the Navy, I know the importance knowing how to maintain project balance while using problem solving to focus on priorities and meeting goals. I also learned the benefit of completing tasks in a timely manner and how to properly handle sensitive material.
